Description

Fractal Puppy is an independent video game studio based in Barcelona, Spain. The team is composed of former colleagues with experience in video game development.

History

Fractal Puppy was created to develope a videogame for a videogame master at UPC as a final master work. While developing Neta Project we developed our own Engine: Fractal Engine which has been used to develop the videogame.
